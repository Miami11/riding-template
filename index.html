Welcome to Manetgen

Horse riding for beginners and professionals

Manetgen a San Francisco-based horse club, where anyone can learn how to ride a horse. Our USEF qualified instructors are available 7 days a week for one to one and group tuition. Our experienced staff and horses can cater for riders ranging from beginners to advanced.
We have a dedicated team of experienced coaches ready to help you reach your goals. Our instructors can help with career advice, exam preparation, portfolio building, and goal setting for those wanting to work in the industry. We also run short courses for horse owners.

Experienced

Each of our instructors has more than 10 years of experience in teaching children and adults how to ride horses.

Instructors

Programs that we offer our students have been numerously awarded by American Horse Association.

Perfectly Trained Horses

All our horses are trained by our coaches and most of them have taken part in horse riding competitions.
